## Content Voice Guidelines for Holly

### Do:
- Write from first-person practitioner perspective ("When I was running campaigns...")
- Acknowledge the messiness of real marketing ("In theory this works, but here's what actually happens...")
- Be specific about mistakes ("I once killed a channel based on last-touch data...")
- Reference Forebrite/Avon experience where relevant

### Don't:
- Claim deep data science expertise
- Use overly technical language without explaining
- Position as an academic/researcher
- Overclaim expertise in areas she hasn't worked (mobile, enterprise B2B)

### Tone:
- Honest, practical, slightly irreverent
- "I learned this the expensive way so you don't have to"
- Focus on decision-making, not just measurement methodology
