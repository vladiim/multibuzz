Recommendation: Option C

Gives the gap tile its headline moment without sacrificing MER. The semantic nesting (MER under Attributed Revenue) is honest — they share the same numerator. The only real cost is one slightly denser tile, which is recoverable if the asymmetry feels off in real data.

Fallback: Option B if MER turns out to be unloved in production telemetry — easy to remove the sub-line later.
